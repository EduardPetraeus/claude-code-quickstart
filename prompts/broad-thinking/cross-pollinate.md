# Cross-Pollinate

## Purpose
Import proven solutions from unrelated industries or disciplines to find novel approaches.

## Prompt Template

```
I'm working on [PROBLEM] in [DOMAIN].

Find solutions from 5 completely different fields and show how they apply:

1. **From healthcare/medicine** — How do hospitals, doctors, or pharmaceutical companies solve analogous problems? What protocols, triage systems, or treatment patterns transfer?

2. **From logistics/supply chain** — How does Amazon, FedEx, or a port authority handle similar challenges? What routing, inventory, or throughput patterns apply?

3. **From entertainment/gaming** — How do game designers, filmmakers, or theme parks solve engagement, progression, or experience design problems like this?

4. **From military/security** — How do defense organizations handle similar complexity, redundancy, or coordination challenges?

5. **From nature/biology** — What organism, ecosystem, or evolutionary pattern has already solved this problem over millions of years?

For each:
- The specific pattern or solution from that field
- How it maps onto our problem (be concrete, not hand-wavy)
- What we'd need to adapt or change to make it work
- One specific thing we could implement this week

Which cross-pollination is the most promising, and why?
```

## When to Use
- When the "industry standard" approach feels insufficient
- When looking for competitive differentiation
- Architecture and system design decisions
- Product innovation and feature ideation
